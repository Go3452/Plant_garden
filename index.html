What is the purpose of the Virtual Herbal Garden?

The Virtual Herbal Garden is designed to provide an interactive and educational platform for users to explore, learn, and understand the medicinal plants used in AYUSH (Ayurveda, Yoga & Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homeopathy) from the comfort of their homes.

How can I interact with the 3D models of the plants?

You can rotate, zoom, and explore each 3D model by using your mouse or touchscreen. This feature allows you to view the plants from different angles and examine their unique characteristics in detail.

Where can I find detailed information about each plant?

Each plant featured in the Virtual Herbal Garden includes a dedicated section with comprehensive details such as its botanical name, common names, habitat, medicinal uses, and cultivation methods. Simply click on a plant to access this information.

Can I search for specific plants in the Virtual Herbal Garden?

Yes, the website includes advanced search and filter options, allowing you to easily locate specific plants based on criteria such as health, medicinal use, flowers, roots and more.

Is there any multimedia content available?

Absolutely! The Virtual Herbal Garden offers high-quality images,3d model, and descriptions for each plant to provide a more immersive and informative experience.

How can I use the information provided on this website?

The information provided is intended for educational purposes. While it can help you learn about the medicinal uses and cultivation of various plants, always consult a qualified healthcare professional before using any plants for medicinal purposes.
